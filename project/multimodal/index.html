Multimodal neuroimaging analysis

Last updated on Mar 19, 2020
PDF

During my thesis work, I conducted analyses involving neuroimaging data (diffusion MRI, fMRI and MRI).

To perform these analyses, I used scripts for data extraction from a large database, ADNI, and used several tools such as SPM, Conn, Freesurfer, FSL, MRtrix, volBrain, TractoFlow, and MRIQC.

Based on what I learned, I am working on tutorials and user-friendly scripts that will soon be available on Github.
